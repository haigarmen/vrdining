DINING IN VR

Course site

This design workshop explores the way in which physical places may intersect, digitally. VR seems the obvious platform for an immersion of this sort, however designing for specific (yet broadly geographic) intersections (for example, the Armenian diaspora) would seem to require a closer analysis of our relationship (the “viewer”’s relationship, that is) to place and perspective.

Workshop Goal

During this two week workshop students were asked to create meaningful immersive virtual reality experiences that connected people together.

Learning Outcomes:

  • using UX methods, create social virtual environments
  • produce virtual experiences using A-Frame web technology
  • integrate 360 Degree Panoramic photography with 3D models to prototype VR environments
